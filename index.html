DAIRY FREE FROZEN DESSERT

WHO
WE ARE

&WHAT WE DO

As we navigate our way through an increasingly animal-free food landscape, we yearn to still indulge our familiar decadent cravings. Bridging the gap between past and the present, we developed Klimon, an almond based, dairy-free frozen dessert that will have consumers thinking they are eating good old fashioned ice cream.

With an array of mouth watering flavors, textures, and inclusions, Klimon satisfies all your ice cream desires without the milk, cream, butter, or eggs! Whether you try Klimon for health and ethical reasons, dietary restrictions, or sustainability issues, just one scoop will have you exclaiming:

Indulgence without sacrifice. A modern solution to tastes of the past. Somewhere a cow is smiling.

"No Dairy.
No Difference."
